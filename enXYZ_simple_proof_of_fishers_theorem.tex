\section{Notation}
Sample $X_1, X_2, \dots, X_n$ is drawn independently from a distribution that
has finite mean and variance, i.e.:
\begin{eqnarray*}
X_i &\sim & i.i.d. \\
E(X_i) &=& E(X) = \mu = \mu_X < \infty \\
Var(X_i) &=& Var(X) = \sigma^2 = \sigma^2_X < \infty
\end{eqnarray*}
